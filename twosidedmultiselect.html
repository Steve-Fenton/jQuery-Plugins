Two Sided Multi Select Summary

The jQuery Two-Sided Multi-Select project is a plugin that converts a multi-select list into a more user-friendly two-sided list with options to move items from the "available" list into the "selected" list and back.

You can move single items or all items back and forth using the buttons, or double click to quick-move any item.

The original order of the items will always be preserved, no matter what order you move them back and forward in.
